Usage Guidelines

Do

  • Use official logos from this brand kit
  • Maintain logo proportions when scaling
  • Use approved color palette
  • Reference the documentary/Herzog story
  • Mention the charity component
  • Keep the philosophical tone
  • Use the penguin emoji 🐧
  • Tag @DERANGEDCOIN on socials

Don't

  • Distort or stretch the logo
  • Change the logo colors
  • Add effects (shadows, gradients) to logo
  • Use on busy/cluttered backgrounds
  • Make false promises about returns
  • Claim official partnership without approval
  • Use for scams or misleading content
